title: "Product Topology"
---

Let $\{X_i\}_{i \in I}$ be a family of topological spaces.

The product topology on

$$
\prod_{i \in I} X_i
$$

is the coarsest topology making every projection map

$$
\pi_j : \prod_{i \in I} X_i \to X_j
$$

continuous.

Equivalently, it is generated by the subbasis

$$
\{\pi_j^{-1}(U) : j \in I,\ U \subseteq X_j \text{ open}\}.
$$

The universal property says that a map

$$
f : Y \to \prod_{i \in I} X_i
$$

is continuous if and only if each component

$$
\pi_j \circ f : Y \to X_j
$$

is continuous.
